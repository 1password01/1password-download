What is 1Password?

1Password helps you manage your login credentials, credit card info, software licenses, and other sensitive data in a secure digital vault. It uses industry-standard AES-256 encryption to keep your information safe. With browser and mobile integrations, you’ll never need to remember another password again.

System Requirements

  • Windows 10 or Windows 11 (64-bit)
  • 4GB RAM minimum
  • Active internet connection
  • A 1Password account (sign-up available during setup)

Why Choose 1Password?

  • Secure encryption: AES-256 bit protection for all your data
  • Password generator: Create strong, unique passwords easily
  • Browser integration: Works with Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari
  • Watchtower: Alerts you to reused, weak, or breached credentials
  • Cross-platform: Sync across Windows, macOS, Android, iOS
